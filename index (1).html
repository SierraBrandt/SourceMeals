What We Do


Finding Meals during the Pandemic

We are committed to assisting people in finding nearby foodbanks, getting food delivered and giving people information on how they can help. 1 in 7 people need food banks to feed their families; with Covid-19, people are loosing their jobs and struggle to find meals to eat.

According to the School Nutrition Accosiation, 20.1 million kids get free lunches at school everyday, that is 68% of all school lunches given to kids. Since the beginning of the pandemic, more and more families have been forced to stay at home and kids are not getting the nutrition they need.

Ways to get involved


Some ways to aid memebers in your community during the pandemic

There are many ways to help food banks during coronavirus. Contact local food banks to ask about volunteering, some pantries are even offering "contactless" volunteering. Money and time is what is most important for food banks, don't be afraid to reach out to your local food bank and ask what they need.

An easy way to help is to use social media to raise awareness. Becoming informed about the need of food banks can raise awareness to others in the comforts of your own home.
